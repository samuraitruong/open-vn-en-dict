  • verb
    -heres; -hered; -hering
    [no obj] :to stick to something :to attach firmly to something
    The stamp failed to adhere.
    usually + to
    The stamp failed to adhere to the envelope.
    The mud adhered to his shoes.
    adhere to
    [phrasal verb]
    adhere to (something) :to act in the way that is required by (something, such as a rule, belief, or promise)
    They will adhere to the terms of the contract.
    Certain standards must be adhered to by all members.
    She adheres to [=follows] a strict vegetarian diet.

    * Các từ tương tự:

  • noun
    plural -tions
    [noncount] :the act of obtaining or removing something from a source :the act of abstracting something
    abstraction of data from hospital records
    formal :a general idea or quality rather than an actual person, object, or event :an abstract idea or quality [count]
    Beauty and truth are abstractions. [noncount]
    the abstraction [=abstract quality] of his ideas
    [noncount] somewhat formal :the state of someone who is not paying attention to what is happening or being said :an abstracted state
    She gazed out the window in abstraction.

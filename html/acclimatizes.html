acclimatize /əˈklaɪməˌtaɪz/  

Đọc giọng UK Đọc giọng US
Anh-Anh
  • verb
    also Brit acclimatise -tizes; -tized; -tizing
    to adjust or adapt to a new climate, place, or situation :acclimate
    usually + to; [no obj]
    The mountain climbers spent a few days acclimatizing [=becoming adjusted] to the high altitude. [+ obj]
    The mountain climbers spent a few days acclimatizing themselves to the high altitude.
